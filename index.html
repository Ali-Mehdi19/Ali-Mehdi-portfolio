About Me

I'm a passionate Full Stack Developer & AI/ML Engineer with a deep expertise in building end-to-end intelligent applications. From crafting beautiful, responsive frontends to architecting robust, scalable backends powered by machine learning — I bridge the gap between design and data science.

My expertise spans across React, React Native, Next.js, FastAPI, and Python for full-stack development, combined with hands-on experience in computer vision (YOLOv11, OpenCV, MediaPipe), natural language processing, and large language model integration using frameworks like LangChain, Ollama, and Hugging Face Transformers.

I specialize in building cloud-native, multi-tenant SaaS platforms with real-time capabilities, integrating AI pipelines for OCR, RAG-based chatbots, data curation, and predictive analytics. Whether it's a mobile app, a web platform, or an AI-powered backend — I deliver production-grade solutions.

Currently focused on pushing the boundaries of AI-driven applications and exploring cutting-edge research in deep learning, transformer architectures, and autonomous systems.
